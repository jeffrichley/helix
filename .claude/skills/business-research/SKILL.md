Use this skill for business, market, competitor, or strategic research tasks.

## Goal

Produce a synthesis grounded in current, attributable evidence rather than unsupported
generalization.

## Working Rules

- define the research question precisely
- prioritize primary and current sources when possible
- separate claims from evidence
- triangulate across multiple sources
- state confidence and uncertainty explicitly

## Evaluation Guidance

- prefer recency when the topic is time-sensitive
- distinguish facts, inference, and speculation
- downgrade conclusions based on low-quality or single-source evidence
- escalate when material decisions rely on weak evidence

## Deliverable Shape

For each research output, include:

- question
- key findings
- supporting evidence
- confidence level
- open uncertainties
- recommended next research steps
